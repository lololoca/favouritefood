Chicken fry can be a delicious and satisfying dish, but it's worth noting some of its nutritional aspects.
Chicken itself is a good source of protein, which is essential for building and repairing tissues in our bodies.
However, the frying process can add extra calories and fat to the dish.
It's important to be mindful of portion sizes and cooking methods to maintain a balanced diet.
